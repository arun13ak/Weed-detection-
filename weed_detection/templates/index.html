This Weed detection AI model is used to identify and differentiate between crops and unwanted weeds in various scenarios.

Different types of weeds

Weeds can cause several problems in agriculture. One of the primary issues is the competition for essential resources like sunlight, water, and nutrients, as weeds tend to compete with crops, leading to decreased crop yields. The reduced yields result from the shading effect of weeds, which inhibits the growth of crops and lowers the overall field productivity. Weeds can also make it challenging to maintain the quality of the harvested crop, as they can contaminate it, making it difficult to separate weed seeds or plant material from the desired crop. Additionally, some weeds can serve as hosts for pests and diseases, which can spread to nearby crops, further impacting agriculture.
